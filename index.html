Decentralized

In the world, there are billions of hardware disks with various processing capabilities that are not fully utilized. For example, a refrigerator repair service company uses a high-capacity server for storing its own and customers' data, but does this server operate at full power during use or not?

The answer is no, because typically a company only uses 40% to 60% of the main capacity, and the rest of the network remains unused, which incurs maintenance and repair costs, making the actual cost of a server almost double. What is the solution and innovation?

Imagine there are several companies in the world facing these problems. Now, at this stage, we are supposed to step in. DHost can cover part of the maintenance costs of such hardware partitions with its unique mechanism worldwide (see the image above). It will be distributed inside various servers and provide high-quality services to customers at a low cost, and using attractive and practical tokenomics, it provides financial assistance for hardware maintenance.
